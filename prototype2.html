Prototype 2

Overview

Prototype 2 was developed using the feedback gathered from the first design. The main objective was to improve the lid fitment, increase the overall strength of the enclosure, and create a more refined and functional case for the ESP32-C3 board.

Improvements Made

  • Adjusted dimensions to improve lid alignment and fit.
  • Added additional clearance to make assembly easier.
  • Improved the overall appearance of the enclosure.
  • Enhanced structural strength around the edges and corners.
  • Maintained easy access to ports and connectors.

Results

The second prototype showed significant improvements compared to the original design. The lid fitted more securely onto the case and was easier to install and remove. The enclosure felt stronger and more durable, making it better suited for everyday use and handling.

Although the design was much closer to the desired outcome, minor adjustments were still identified before producing the final version. These included refining the overall finish and making small dimensional changes to ensure the best possible fit and appearance.
